Content

Home page

Introduction: I have always struggled writing resumes. The purpose of writing a resume is to try and convince someone that they should give you a job by showing them how your experience and education qualify you for the position, but you are only allowed one page of space. How do you fit a lifetime of experience and education in one page of space? The answer is you don’t. It’s impossible. So you end up trying to read the mind of the potential employer and include only the things that they will want to see.

About Me: I am an American Expatriate living in the Las Palmas province of Spain with my wife( a Spanish citizen), my daughter and father-in-law. I have worked in a variety of Jobs over the course of my life from mowing lawns as a teenager to leading marines as a marine corporal in Iraq. I have earned a bachelor's degree and I am working on my second. I have been working in the tech industry as a web developer for the last five years but found I was lacking some skill that would enable me to progress.

Soft Skills: Hardworking Quicklearner Problem Solving Communication Skills Teamwork Leadership
