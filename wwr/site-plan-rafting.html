Overview

Purpose

Make the sport of white water rafting accessable to everyone regardless of physical or mental abilities. A group of experience Whitewater rafters want to share the sport they love and the beauty of Americas wild places with those who normally can't participate due to physical or mental limitations.

Audience

People with disabilities and their family or friends, who want to do and experience more than they normally can due to their limits placed on them by their disabilities. Those that want to experience the thrill of a Whitewater rafting adventure and experience the beauty of americas wild rivers.
